# Functionalities: Major Features and Modules of the Proposed System

## 4.1 User Authentication and Identity Management

This module serves as the foundational security layer of the system, ensuring that all access to the platform is verified and governed by strict organizational roles. It solves the problem of unauthorized data access and maintains accountability across the management lifecycle by distinguishing between administrative staff and passengers.

*   **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC):** Implements distinct permission tiers for Administrators, Staff, and Passengers to ensure users interact only with authorized features.
*   **Secure Session Management:** Employs token-based authentication to maintain stateful user sessions across the decentralized web environment.
*   **Encrypted Credential Storage:** Utilizes advanced hashing algorithms for protecting user passwords, ensuring data privacy and compliance with security standards.

## 4.2 Flight Scheduling and Operational Control

The Flight Scheduling module is the core engine for airline logistics, allowing for the precise management of global flight routes and timings. It replaces fragmented scheduling methods with a centralized digital timeline, ensuring that flight information remains consistent across all passenger and staff interfaces.

*   **Dynamic Route Planning:** Facilitates the creation and modification of flight paths, including the definition of departure and arrival coordinates.
*   **Real-Time Status Synchronization:** Allows for the instantaneous update of flight states (e.g., Scheduled, Boarding, or Delayed) to reflect live operational conditions.
*   **Automated Validation:** Ensures that flight timing and aircraft assignments do not overlap or conflict with existing operational schedules.

## 4.3 Aircraft Fleet and Asset Management

This module provides a comprehensive digital ledger for tracking the airline's physical assets. It allows the organization to monitor the health and capacity of its fleet, solving the challenge of manual inventory tracking and ensuring that aircraft are utilized efficiently across different routes.

*   **Asset Registration:** Enables the detailed logging of aircraft specifications, including model type, registration identity, and passenger capacity.
*   **Status Monitoring:** Tracks the operational readiness of each unit, identifying aircraft that are currently active, in maintenance, or grounded.
*   **Assignment Linkage:** Bridges the gap between hardware and operations by allowing specific aircraft units to be assigned to scheduled flight routes.

## 4.4 Passenger Search and Reservation Engine

The Reservation Engine is a user-centric module designed to streamline the consumer journey from flight discovery to ticket issuance. It automates the booking process, eliminating the need for manual intervention and providing passengers with a reliable self-service interface.

*   **Interactive Flight Search:** Enables passengers to query the operational database for available flights based on their specific travel coordinates.
*   **Real-Time Availability Check:** Continuously monitors flight capacity to ensure that seat bookings are only performed for available inventory.
*   **Digital Boarding Pass Generation:** Automates the creation of unique booking references and flight records, allowing passengers to access their itineraries instantly via the "My Journeys" portal.

## 4.5 Crew Assignment and Personnel Deployment

This module optimizes human resource allocation by linking qualified personnel to scheduled flight operations. It solves the logistical complexity of crew scheduling, ensuring that every flight meets regulatory and operational staffing requirements before departure.

*   **Personnel Deployment:** Facilitates the assignment of pilots and cabin crew members to specific flight IDs based on their roles and availability.
*   **Duty Tracking:** Maintains a record of active assignments for all staff members, preventing over-scheduling and improving personnel management.
*   **Crew Communication:** Provides a dedicated interface for staff to receive their operational briefings and schedule updates in real-time.

## 4.6 System Analytics and Business Intelligence

The Analytics module provides the executive layer of the system with actionable insights derived from live operational data. It processes large volumes of transactional information into visual metrics, enabling data-driven decision-making for airline management.

*   **Live Operational Telemetry:** Visualizes real-time metrics such as total active flights, booking conversion rates, and overall passenger volume.
*   **Revenue Analytics:** Automatically calculates financial performance based on confirmed ticket bookings, providing a clear view of organizational growth.
*   **Operational Reporting:** Generates statistical distributions of flight statuses and fleet utilization, helping administrators identify and respond to operational bottlenecks.
